# EPT-Post-Conflict
The Energy Planning Tool for Post-Conflict settings (EPT-Post-Conflict) has been developed by KTH Royal Institute of Technology in collaboration with the United Nations Office for Project Services (UNOPS). The tool aims to help energy planners assess energy demands needed to attain sustainable post-conflict development and to navigate energy rebuilding in specific local contexts.

**The tool is currently under development and will be made available soon.** In the file EPT-PCv0 - CONTENTS there is a preview of the tool available.

The research work was funded by the Formas grant no. 2024-01075
